PixieCast Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 15, 2026

PixieCast ("the app") is developed and operated by Garrett Smith ("we," "us"). This policy explains what information the app handles and how. The short version: **PixieCast does not collect, store, or sell your personal information.** There are no accounts, no sign-ins, and no ads.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, or any account credentials.
- We do not access your device's location.
- We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.
- We do not use advertising networks or sell data to third parties.
- We do not use third-party analytics trackers.

## Information Handled by the App

### Weather, radar, and crowd data
The app fetches public data over the internet to show you forecasts and park information. These requests are anonymous — they do not include any personal information about you:

- **Open-Meteo** (open-meteo.com) — weather forecasts for fixed park coordinates
- **National Weather Service** (api.weather.gov) — active weather alerts for the Walt Disney World area
- **Queue-Times** (queue-times.com) — live ride wait times
- **RainViewer** (rainviewer.com) — weather radar tiles

Like any internet service, these providers may see your device's IP address as part of routine request handling. We do not send them anything else.

### Trip information
If you set up a trip in the app (dates, park assignments, party size, resort), that information is stored **only on your device**. It is never uploaded to us or anyone else. Deleting the trip in the app, or deleting the app, removes it.

### Purchases and subscriptions
Premium subscriptions are processed by **Apple** through the App Store; we never see your payment details. To manage subscription status, the app uses **RevenueCat** (revenuecat.com), which assigns your device a random anonymous identifier and records your purchase status. This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or identity. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

## Children's Privacy

PixieCast is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Because we don't collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on our end. All app data (trip details, subscription status cache) lives on your device and is removed when you delete the app. To cancel a subscription, use your App Store account settings.

If we change this policy, we will update the effective date above and publish the new version at this same address.
